The CODEX One Care model is designed to ensure continuity of care, operational efficiency, and improved patient outcomes through structured coordination and digital systems.

## Core Components

### 1. Care Coordination
- Structured patient journey from entry to discharge and follow-up
- Referral and handover tracking
- Follow-up and continuity planning

### 2. Digital Health Infrastructure
- Electronic Medical Records (EMR)
- Appointment and workflow tracking
- Clinical documentation support
- Data capture and reporting

### 3. Operations & Finance Integration
- Department-level cost tracking
- Revenue and expenditure visibility
- Operational performance insights
- Support for audits and planning

### 4. Patient Experience
- Reduced waiting times
- Clear communication
- Consistent care pathways
- Feedback and satisfaction monitoring

## Delivery Approach

CODEX One Care is implemented through facility assessment, system setup, staff training, and ongoing support to ensure sustainable adoption and continuous improvement.
