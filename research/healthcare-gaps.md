# CODEX One Care – Healthcare Gaps Research

## Key Challenges in Healthcare Facilities

1. Fragmented patient records leading to repeated tests
2. Poor care continuity, especially for chronic patients
3. Inefficient workflows causing long waiting times
4. Limited visibility into operational and financial performance
5. Weak data collection, analysis, and reporting systems

## Opportunities

- Implement coordinated care pathways
- Digitize patient records and workflow management
- Integrate operational and financial monitoring tools
- Provide actionable insights for facility decision-making
- Improve patient satisfaction and outcomes
