## Revenue Streams

1. **Setup & Onboarding Fee**
   - One-time fee for system setup, workflow mapping, and staff onboarding.

2. **Monthly Subscription**
   - Tiered pricing based on facility size and number of active users.

3. **Training & Capacity-Building**
   - Optional workshops and ongoing staff training.

4. **Data & Reporting Services**
   - Advanced analytics, audit support, and operational insights.

5. **Custom Integrations / Consulting**
   - Facility-specific digital integrations or specialized consulting.
